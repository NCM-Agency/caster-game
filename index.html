Step Into the Arena

Cross the threshold of the Arcanium, a sacred arena that hosts the legendary competition for the Caster's Cup! Choose one of seven Casters to battle for this coveted prize and harness ancient forces to prove your power. Reign victorious by expertly channeling your favored forms of magic. Or let knowledge guide your way by strategically unlocking new abilities and spells to surpass your opponents. No matter your approach, one thing is certain… only one can claim the Caster's Cup!

Choose Your Caster

Zok Kashto

Immortal Priest

Once a revered priest, Zok Kashto was bound as the eternal guardian of his Pharaoh's tomb. After millennia of solitude, his hunger to unleash his magic grew. He ensnared a grave robber to bear his curse, and now he emerges to fight in the Arcanium.

Lin Min

Sacred Guardian

After a long and peaceful life protecting the enchantments in her ancestral forest, Lin Min emerges to avenge a stolen artifact and restore her family's magic in the arena. Will winning the Caster's Cup restore balance in her homeland?

Killian

Shackled Revenant

A formidable force lies inside Killian. Years of solitude and discipline taught him to control this energy, imprisoning it within a lattice of molten tattoos that swirl over his body. But with his mana now waning, he turns to the arena in search of new power.

Tobias

Time Thief

An insatiable curiosity and a lust for power landed inventor Tobias under the power of a demonic master. Now Tobias must travel through time to acquire magical artifacts for his keeper until he procures a source of magic powerful enough to escape enslavement.

Ravaress

Maniacal Necromancer

Orphaned by a hunting expedition gone awry, young Ravaress found solace in the voices of the dead. Over time, their whispers sharpened her power, granting her command of rotting flesh and a magic both feared and forbidden.

Nomi

Charming Rebel

Decades of enchanting in the streets near the Arcanium have made Nomi the people's Caster. Though most know Nomi's exuberance and warm heart, the selfish and greedy have learned to fear the lightning behind their eyes.

Devaedra

Demonic Zealot

As a devout servant of The Pantheon, Devaedra attracted a mysterious mentor that developed her arcane powers and gave her great influence. After abusing her power, she was cast out of the holy order. Now she enters the arena, burning for revenge and the power once hers.

Who We Are

Not Corrupt Games was founded by a group of friends that met in the video game industry. Sick of screens, we decided to bring our love of world-building and gameplay to in-person tabletop experiences that foster meaningful connections through friendly competition. We're real people that want to make cool stuff with creators we love. Come play with us!
